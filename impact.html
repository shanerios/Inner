Impact & Funding

Inner is a digital audio arts project—composed for clarity, regulation, and depth.

This page is designed for grantmakers, arts organizations, and partners reviewing Inner as an evolving body of audio work.

Core Identity

Inner is a digital audio arts experience that uses sound, guided narrative, and immersive environments to support mental clarity, emotional regulation, and expanded states of awareness.

It exists at the intersection of art, sound design, and self-guided healing—offering accessible experiences that help individuals reconnect with themselves.

Expanded Description

Inner is an evolving digital audio arts project that blends sound design, voice, and interactive environments into guided experiences that support emotional well-being, self-awareness, and mental clarity.

The project presents a series of immersive Chambers and sound-based journeys—each designed as a standalone artistic work guiding participants through states of reflection, grounding, and expanded awareness.

Rooted in principles of ritual, repetition, and sonic immersion, Inner reimagines how art can be experienced in a modern, accessible format—transforming a mobile device into a space for intentional pause, inner exploration, and restoration.

While delivered digitally, Inner is fundamentally an artistic practice: each experience is composed with intention—combining voice performance, spatial sound, and symbolic environments to create a cohesive emotional and psychological journey.

The project is designed to grow as a living body of work, expanding with new compositions, collaborations, and culturally influenced soundscapes over time.

Public Benefit

Inner provides accessible tools for emotional regulation, stress reduction, and self-reflection through art.

In a time where many individuals lack access to traditional wellness resources, Inner offers a low-barrier, on-demand experience that can be accessed privately and without cost barriers in its foundational form.

Accessibility

Mobile-first design with low-cost / free entry experiences.

Non-clinical healing

Emotional well-being supported through art-based methods.

Inclusive participation

Open to participants regardless of background or prior experience.

Future phases include free public releases of select audio works, community activations and workshops, and collaborations with artists and voice practitioners—including those based in Puerto Rico.

Artistic Framing

Each Inner experience is treated as a composed piece of audio art, created through a layered process involving:

  • Voice performance (guided narration)
  • Sound design and frequency-based composition
  • Environmental storytelling through audio and visual tone

Rather than passive listening, participants are guided through structured internal experiences—making the audience an active participant in the work itself.

This positions Inner within a lineage of sound art, experimental audio, and ritual-based artistic practice—translated into a contemporary digital format.

Cultural & Puerto Rico Alignment

A core direction of Inner includes collaboration with artists and sound designers from Puerto Rico—integrating natural soundscapes, cultural textures, and local creative voices into future works.

  • Field recordings (e.g., coquí frogs, ocean, natural environments)
  • Bilingual voice work (English / Spanish)
  • Partnerships with local artists and creators

These collaborations aim to support local creative economies while ensuring the work reflects a diverse and grounded cultural presence.

Funding Use

Funding will support the creation of new audio works within the Inner series, including:

  • Compensation for voice artists (including bilingual performers)
  • Sound design and composition by collaborating artists
  • Development of new guided experiences (Chambers)
  • Expansion of free, publicly accessible content

Additional support enables fair pay for artists, higher production quality and accessibility, and broader distribution of the work to public audiences.
