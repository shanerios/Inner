About Inner

A practice for the threshold.

Inner is a guided audio and learning app for lucid dreaming, dream recall, sleep-state awareness, deep rest, and out-of-body exploration. It is built as a practice to return to—not a feed to consume.

One system, several ways in.

Chambers

Immersive guided-audio environments designed for settling attention, deep rest, and progressive inner practice.

Lessons

Structured learning paths for dream recall, lucid awareness, threshold states, OBE practice, and integration.

Guardians & Witnesses

Inner's native frameworks for felt awareness within dreams and steadiness through the OBE crossing. Guardian 1 — Recognition: Cultivation is currently available; the remaining Guardian audio system is in development.

Journal & Intentions

Tools for recording dreams, noticing recurring signals, and carrying a clear orientation into sleep.

Books

Auditory Ascension develops the theory behind the Chambers and Separation Witnesses. Guardians contains the complete theoretical foundation of the Guardian Ecology.

Repetition

Inner is designed around return. Familiarity reduces novelty and makes subtler changes in attention easier to recognize.

Created by Shane Rios.

Inner was founded and created by Shane Rios as an evolving body of original guided voice, sound design, learning material, and inner-practice frameworks. The app and books belong to the same work: experience and explanation, practice and theory.

Inner welcomes spiritual, symbolic, psychological, and phenomenological interpretations of inner experience without requiring users to adopt one fixed explanation.

What Inner is—and is not.

Inner is a self-guided contemplative and educational practice. It does not promise instant lucid dreams, guaranteed out-of-body experiences, medical outcomes, or a substitute for healthy sleep or professional care.

The invitation is simpler: listen carefully, practice consistently, protect your rest, record what happens, and let experience develop without forcing it.
