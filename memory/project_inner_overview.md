Inner is a React Native (Expo) audio-engineered experience app focused on:
- Lucid dreaming and OBE (out-of-body experience) induction
- Dream logging
- Meditation and relaxation

Key tech: RevenueCat (subscriptions/paywall), PostHog (analytics), Expo Updates (OTA).

Brand terms: "Sanctum Sphere", "Inner Resonance" — intentional, do not flag as repetition.

Planned future feature: AI Guide — not just an information index, but a personalized guide to help users with their lucid dreaming/OBE/meditation journey, also serving as a library for in-app lessons.

**Why:** Enhance the guided experience beyond static audio/lessons; make the app feel like a personal mentor.
**How to apply:** When suggesting features or architecture, consider how they integrate with the existing lesson system and audio-first UX.
