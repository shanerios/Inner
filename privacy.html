Privacy Policy for Inner

Effective Date: July 23, 2025

At Inner, your privacy is deeply valued. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Inner app.

1. Data We Collect

Inner is designed to honor your peace and privacy. We do not collect personal data unless you explicitly opt into features that require it.

  • Onboarding Preferences: Your selected intentions and preferences are stored locally on your device for a personalized experience. They are not transmitted or stored externally.
  • Optional Features: If future features such as account creation, cloud backup, or synced experiences are added, you will be informed and prompted for consent before any data is used.

2. How We Use Your Data

All user data (such as selected intentions) is used strictly to enhance your personal experience within the app. It is not shared, sold, or used for advertising purposes.

3. Offline-First Design

Inner is designed to function primarily offline. This reduces third-party tracking and respects your digital space.

4. No Cookies or Ads

We do not use cookies or display third-party advertisements in the Inner app.

5. Analytics

We may use anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (if implemented in the future) to improve the app. No personally identifiable data is ever collected without your knowledge.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information stored locally (e.g. your intentions)
  • Reset or delete your local data through the app settings (if provided)
  • Decline any future features that require external data

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update this page and notify users through the app if the changes are significant.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy or this policy, you may contact us at:

Email: support@getinner.app
