Privacy Policy for Inner

Effective Date: October 27, 2025

Inner is designed as a quiet space. Your privacy is not just respected — it is foundational to the experience. This policy explains what is collected, what is not, and how your data is handled.

1. What We Collect

Inner collects minimal data, and only what is necessary to provide and improve the experience.

  • On-device data: Your intentions, preferences, journal entries, downloaded audio, and display name are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to external servers.
  • Usage analytics (anonymous): We collect limited, non-identifying information about how the app is used (such as feature interactions and navigation patterns) to understand what is working and how to improve Inner.
  • Error reporting: In the event of a crash or malfunction, technical information may be collected to help diagnose and fix issues.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • No personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or identity) is collected without your explicit input.
  • No data is sold, rented, or used for advertising.
  • No tracking across other apps or services.

3. How Data Is Used

Any data collected is used solely to:

  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Understand feature usage and refine the experience
  • Guide future development decisions

4. Offline-First Design

Inner is designed to function primarily offline. This reduces reliance on external services and helps maintain your privacy.

5. Analytics

We use privacy-conscious analytics tools to understand general usage patterns within the app. This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you.

Analytics may include interactions such as screen views, feature usage, and session behavior.

Where possible, you may opt out of analytics through app settings.

6. Third-Party Services

Inner uses trusted third-party services for analytics and error monitoring. These services may process anonymous usage and technical data to help improve the app.

These services do not receive personally identifiable information and are used strictly for functionality and improvement.

7. Your Control

You remain in control of your experience. You may:

  • Clear or reset your local data within the app (if available)
  • Choose whether to engage with optional features
  • Opt out of analytics where applicable

8. Data Security

Your data is protected by your device’s built-in security. We do not maintain external databases of your personal information.

9. Data Retention

Data stored locally remains on your device until you choose to remove it. We do not retain personal data externally.

10. Future Features

If Inner introduces features such as accounts, cloud sync, or expanded data functionality, you will be clearly informed and asked for consent before any data is collected or used.

11. Changes to This Policy

This policy may evolve as Inner evolves. If changes are significant, you will be notified within the app.
